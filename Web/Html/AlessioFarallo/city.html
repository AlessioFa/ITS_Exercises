My city: Trento

An alpine city rich in heritage and natural beauty

Nestled between its unmistakable mountains, Trento boasts a millennial history and has managed to preserve its ancient splendor over time. Rich in excellent museums and cultural sites, Trento is an alpine city of rare beauty where art and culture meet stunning landscapes and unspoiled nature. Among its top-tier museums are the Museo delle Scienze di Trento, designed by Renzo Piano, the Castello del Buonconsiglio, the Underground Archaeological Space of Roman Tridentum, the Early Christian Basilica located beneath the Cathedral of San Vigilio, the Diocesan Museum of Trento, the Civic Gallery of Trento - Mart, the Piedicastello Galleries, the Gianni Caproni Aeronautics Museum, and many others.

Anyone visiting Trento will certainly associate the city's name with the famous Council, the historical event that took place between 1545 and 1563, making the city renowned worldwide. What was decided during those years marked European history like few other events, representing a true turning point with huge consequences on social life, art, architecture, and music.
