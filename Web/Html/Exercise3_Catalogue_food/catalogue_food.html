Spaghetti

Spaghetti. The word rolls off the tongue almost as deliciously as it tastes. Perfect for every mood – from a cosy lunch for one to a lively meal out – spaghetti infatuates foodies worldwide. And as if you needed one, National Spaghetti Day (4 January annually) presents the perfect excuse to gorge on these long, round-shaped noodle strands of Sicilian origin. To celebrate everyone’s favourite pasta, we uncover a little history behind spaghetti as well as the best places to taste it in Italy.

Steak

Steak: A delicious cut of meat, typically grilled, known for its rich flavor and tender texture. Enjoyed worldwide, from casual grills to fine dining experiences, steak can be cooked in various styles, from rare to well-done.

Salmon

A rich and flavorful fish, prized for its tender texture and versatility in the kitchen. Whether grilled, baked, or smoked, salmon is a healthy and delicious option for any meal. Known for its high omega-3 fatty acid content, it’s both nutritious and satisfying, making it a popular choice among seafood lovers worldwide.

Vegetables

A refreshing mix of fresh vegetables, often paired with a variety of dressings and toppings. Salads can be enjoyed as a light starter or a main course, and are a versatile option for a healthy meal. From crisp greens to vibrant vegetables, salads offer a wide range of flavors and textures, making them a favorite choice for those seeking a nutritious and satisfying dish.

Fruits

A natural and healthy source of essential vitamins and minerals. With their wide variety of flavors, textures, and colors, fruits offer a refreshing and nutritious addition to any meal. From sweet and juicy to tangy and refreshing, fruits provide a delicious way to boost your health and satisfy your cravings.
